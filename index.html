Welcome to the Produce Flow Tracker!

Produce Flow refers to the global flow of produce across countries and regions and how these flows may expose unsustainable growing or unethical labor practices. Through this series of visualizations, we have created an invaluable analytics tool to target and reform environmental hazards exposed by investigating suspicious trading patterns.
