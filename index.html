Welcome to the Produce Flow Tracker!

Produce Flow refers to the global flow of produce across countries and regions. Through this series of visualizations, we have created an invaluable analytics tool to study individual crop flows or country flows to allow users to explore data provided by the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations. Chart color palettes provided by Microsoft.
